CET Online Examination Instructions

Please read the instructions carefully

  • Total duration of examination is 180 minutes.
  • The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer will display remaining time.
  • The Question Palette displayed on the right side will show the status of each question.
  • You can navigate between questions using the palette.
  • Click Save & Next to save your answer.
  • Click Mark for Review if you want to review later.
  • Click Submit Exam once you finish the test.
  • Do not refresh the browser during the exam.
  • The exam will automatically submit when time expires.
